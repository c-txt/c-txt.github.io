## About

Writing serves to clarify thought, and the writings on this site are primarily to clarify my own thinking as I study my favorite book, the Bible. Secondarily, I also hope they will be of some use to anyone else on the same glorious journey of discovering the treasues and truths about Jesus Christ and the Christian life that are hidden throughout this wonderful book.

My writings and jottings will as a matter of course be riddled with errors as I am merely a student. And so, if you disagree with anything on this site, or are able to offer constructive criticism or corrections, I welcome you to reach out to me through the Contact page.

Thanks for dropping in and hope this site is a blessing to you in some way or the other!
