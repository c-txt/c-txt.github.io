# Participles Overview

|   |  Stem |  Connecting Vowel / Tense Formative |  Participle Morpheme (m-f-n) |  Case Ending (m-f-n) |
|---|---|---|---|---|
|  PaP |  Present |  o |  ντ-υσα-ντ |  3-1-3 |
|  PmP & PpP |  Present |  o |  μενο-μενη-μενο |  2-1-2 |
|  --- |  --- |  --- |  --- |  --- |
|  AaP-1 |  Unaugmented 1st Aorist Active/Middle |  σα |  ντ-σα-ντ |  3-1-3 |
|  AmP-1 |  Unaugmented 1st Aorist Active/Middle |  σα |  μενο-μενη-μενο |  2-1-2 |
|  ApP-1 |  Unaugmented 1st Aorist Passive |  θ**ε** |  ντ-ισα-ντ |  3-1-3 |
|  --- |  --- |  --- |  --- |  --- |
|  AaP-2 |  Unaugmented 2nd Aorist Active/MIddle |  ο |  ντ-υσα-ντ |  3-1-3 |
|  AmP-2 |  Unaugmented 2nd Aorist Active/Middle |  ο |  μενο-μενη-μενο |  2-1-2 |
|  ApP-2 |  Unaugmented 2nd Aorist Passive |  θ**ε** |  ντ-ισα-ντ |  3-1-3 |
|    ---    |  --- |  --- |  --- |  --- |
|    RaP    |  Reduplicated Perfect Active |  κ |  οτ-υια-οτ |  3-1-3 |
|    RmP & RpP    |  Reduplicated Perfect Middle/Passive |  - |  μενο-μενη-μενο |  2-1-2 |


Note: For PaP, the nom. sg. has an exception

Note: For RaP, the nom. sg. has an exception

Note: For ApP-1 and ApP-2, the tense formative is 'θε' and not 'θη'

Note: Middles are always 2-1-2 declensions; in the Presents and Perfects even the passives are in 2-1-2.

Note: The Aorists passive participles use a 3-1-3 pattern
