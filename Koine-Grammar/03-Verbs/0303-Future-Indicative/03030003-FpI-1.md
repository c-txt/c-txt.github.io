
# 1st Future Passive Indicative

1FpI: Aorist Passive Tense Stem (without augment) + Tense Formative (θησ) + Connecting Vowel + Primary Passive Personal Endings

|   |  Stem | Tense Formative |  Conn. Vowel | Ending | Outcome |
|---|---|--|---|--|--|
|  1st Sg | λύ | θησ | ο | μαι | λύθησομαι |
|  2nd Sg | λύ | θησ | ε | σαι | λύθησῃ |
|  3rd Sg | λύ | θησ | ε | ται | λύθησεται |
|   |   |  |   |  |  |
|  1st Pl | λύ | θησ | ό | μεθα | λύθησόμεθα |
|  2nd Pl | λύ | θησ | ε | σθε | λύθησεσθε |
|  3rd Pl | λύ | θησ | ο | νται | λύθησονται |

Note: In 2nd Sg the σ drops because it comes between two vowels.
Note: Unlike the 1st and 2nd Aorist Passives, the 1st and 2nd Future Passives use Primary **Passive** Endings, probably because it uses a borrowed Aorist stem and does not have a passive stem of its own.
