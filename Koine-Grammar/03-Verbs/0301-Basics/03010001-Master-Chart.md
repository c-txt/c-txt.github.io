**Notes (on tense formatives):**

- The Presents don't use a tense formative but use a connecting vowel
- The Futures use both a tense formative and a connecting vowel
- The Aorists use either a tense formative or a connecting vowel
- The Perfects use a tense formative in the Active and no tense formative in middle/passive; but never use the connecting vowel

Notes (on augments):
- All tenses that occur in the past use an augment or reduplications
- The Imperfects are like the presents except for the augment

**Notes (on personal endings):**
- The future and the present and the perfect always use the primary endings
- The imperfect and the aorist always use the secondary endings
